About Spirit

Engineering Healthcare, Pushing frontiers

Spirit is an annual all India student's conclave and a national seminar organised by the Pharmaceutical Society of IIT (BHU) Varanasi. We are organising Spirit for the sixth time in the year 2015 along with new avenues for development, creativity, fun and learning. The event provides you opportunity to get yourself indulged in the technical cum scientific advancement that the world is gaining of late in pharmaceutical industry. Not only this, a sojourn to the holy city of Varanasi will give you an amalgamation of cultural, spiritual and divine atmosphere leading to a salubrious state of your psyche. As it is rightly said " A healthy mind resides in a healthy body. " We wish you a wonderful time and enthralling experience, should you wish to be there at the holy city and paying visit to your own Spirit.....

NATIONAL SEMINAR

" Frontiers of Pharmaceutical Science & Technology "

Today every field of study is developing with an unprecedented speed and the same is true for the pharmaceutical sciences. It becomes important for the student of pharmacy and healthcare sciences at large to be abreast of the current trends and developments regarding the related fields because health care of the population is the most important asset of a progressing and prosperous country. This seminar aims to make the students of various pharmaceutical or medical institutes and research centers, attending Spirit’15, aware about the emerging trends in the pharmaceutical sciences and technology, giving them an opportunity to converse with the industrial technical experts; providing them with first-hand information about various technologies from people in the industry working on them. The lectures in the seminar would be delivered mostly by Industrial Scientists and other experts in their relevant fields in Pharmaceutical Research covering the above topic. This would act as a new learning experience for the attending students and also increase their awareness about the new developments happening in the industry. We will be updating our speakers on confirmation.

The land of Kashi welcomes all knowledge-seekers with open arms of sunrise over the Ganges amidst azaan in mosques. The harbinger of faith in this part of the universe. The only reason mankind is alive is due to its unending quest for knowledge. We gainknowledge to make our lives better. Health is an important aspect of all our lives. The department of Pharmaceutical Sciences and Technology has seen several phases of growthsince its inception in 1919. We welcome you to a journey of knowledge, legacy and fun.

Healthcare industry is growing at an unprecedented pace. We need quality professionals to cater to the ever rising demand. The newer diseases, development of generic analogues, better targeting, minimising toxicity and side effects while maximising efficacy. All of this requires the best minds and resources. The Indian Institute of Technology is haven for all such frontier-level research. Witness one of the country's oldest technological institution, its legacy and its aura.

Spirit is the annual pharma festival of the IITs. The torchbearer department incepted an event under the name, invoking participation nationwide. Spirit boasts of hundreds of participants every year. The event has two dimensions, a student-participatory section of events under the categories-Scientific, Literary and Business; and a National Seminar.

Participate in events with full fervour like you have got nothing to lose. Actually it is true because whether you win or not a specific competition, one thing that no one can deny you is the priceless experience you will gain, the memories you will cherish and the acquaintances you might make.
